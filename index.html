What is Fiesta?

At the core we are an online rsvp website and digital invitation builder. But Fiesta is not just that, Fiesta builds tools that will enhance and organise your wedding experience for you and your guests. All in real time. See a few of our tools below:

Online RSVP management

Anything you can imagine you need to know about your guest, like what meal do they want? what event are they attending? Do they have a song to suggest? you get the idea. Jazzy

Create a registry from any website

Create your gift registry with any online retailer. Simply enter the url and we’ll do all the magic to make it happen. We also support multiple registries.

Design your dream invite, online

Create an entirely custom invite for your guests to view. Start from one of our 30 modern templates and customise until your hearts content! Add custom pages, content, directions and much more.

Build a social album of your day

Engage with your guests with our social wedding wall. Create your hastag and watch your friends send love via Facebook, Instagram & Twitter or good ol’ fashioned photo upload. We’ll also save them in a gallery for you to view after the fun.
